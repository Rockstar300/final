Choosing a Betta Fish

When selecting a Betta, look for a fish that: - Has vibrant colors and clean, undamaged fins. - Is active and alert, swimming around the tank. - Doesn’t show signs of illness (e.g., faded color, lethargy, unusual behavior).

Welcome fellow fish lovers!

Betta fish are native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, and Laos. They are found in slow-moving waters like rice paddies, ponds, and shallow streams.
